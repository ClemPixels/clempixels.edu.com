Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know the right courses for me?

We recommend checking out our courses based on your interests, skill level, and goals. We also provide personalized recommendations based on your past learning experiences.

What are the campus hours and locations?

Our campus hours and locations vary by semester and building. Please visit our Campus Information page for the most up-to-date information.

What are your admission requirements?

Our admission requirements vary by program, but generally include a high school diploma, transcripts, and standardized test scores. Please visit our Admissions page for more information.

How do I apply for financial aid or scholarships?

To apply for financial aid or scholarships, please complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) and review our scholarship opportunities on our Financial Aid page.

How do I get involved in extracurricular activities?

We offer a variety of extracurricular activities and clubs. Please visit our Student Life page to learn more and get involved.

What are the tuition and fees for your programs?

Our tuition and fees vary by program and semester. Please visit our Tuition and Fees page for the most up-to-date information.

Can I take online courses or earn a degree online?

Yes, we offer a variety of online courses and degree programs. Please visit our Online Learning page to explore our options.

How do I access my student records or transcripts?

You can access your student records and transcripts through our student portal or by contacting our Registrar's Office.

What support services do you offer for students?

We offer a range of support services, including academic advising, counseling, and tutoring. Please visit our Student Support page to learn more.

Can I transfer credits from another institution?

Yes, we accept transfer credits from accredited institutions. Please visit our Transfer Credits page to learn more about our transfer policy.
